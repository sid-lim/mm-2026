# 2026 NCAA March Madness Bracket - Design Ideas

## Approach 1: Sports Broadcast Dark Mode
<response>
<text>
**Design Movement:** ESPN/Sports Broadcast Dark Dashboard

**Core Principles:**
- High-contrast dark background with vivid accent colors for readability under any lighting
- Data-forward layout: stats and numbers are the hero, not decorative elements
- Bold typography hierarchy inspired by sports broadcast graphics
- Bracket lines as structural design elements, not just connectors

**Color Philosophy:** Deep navy/charcoal background (#0D1117) with electric blue (#00A3FF) and gold (#FFB800) accents. Red (#FF3B3B) for upsets/alerts. The palette evokes a premium sports broadcast feel — authoritative and exciting.

**Layout Paradigm:** Full-width bracket visualization as the primary canvas, with a collapsible stats sidebar. The bracket flows left-to-right with the championship at center. Stats panels slide in from the right on team selection.

**Signature Elements:**
1. Glowing seed number badges (electric blue for 1-seeds, gold for predicted winners)
2. Animated bracket connector lines that pulse when a winner is selected
3. KenPom efficiency bars displayed inline with each team matchup

**Interaction Philosophy:** Click any matchup to expand detailed stats. Hover reveals win probability. Selected winners glow and propagate up the bracket.

**Animation:** Smooth slide transitions for bracket progression. Subtle particle effects in the background. Stats bars animate in on load.

**Typography System:** "Barlow Condensed" for team names and headings (bold, sporty), "IBM Plex Mono" for statistics (technical precision), "Barlow" for body text.

## Approach 2: Newspaper Sports Section
<response>
<text>
**Design Movement:** Broadsheet Sports Journalism / Editorial

**Core Principles:**
- Editorial grid layout inspired by sports newspaper front pages
- Ink-on-paper texture with high typographic contrast
- Data presented as infographics within editorial columns
- Bracket as a centerpiece "pull-out" section

**Color Philosophy:** Off-white (#F5F0E8) background with deep black (#1A1A1A) text and a single accent color — tournament orange (#E85D04). Minimal color use creates a premium editorial feel.

**Layout Paradigm:** Asymmetric newspaper grid. Bracket occupies 60% of viewport. Analysis columns flank it. Headlines in large serif type break up sections.

**Signature Elements:**
1. Halftone dot patterns in section headers
2. Pull-quote style stat callouts with thick left borders
3. "Upset Alert" stamps in red ink style

**Interaction Philosophy:** Clicking teams reveals editorial-style analysis in a side column. Bracket updates like a newspaper being reprinted.

**Animation:** Typewriter effect for analysis text. Ink-spread transitions between sections.

**Typography System:** "Playfair Display" for headlines (authoritative serif), "Source Serif 4" for body, "Roboto Mono" for stats.
</text>

## Approach 3: Modern Sports Analytics Dashboard (CHOSEN)
<response>
<text>
**Design Movement:** Sports Analytics / Data Visualization Studio

**Core Principles:**
- Clean white/light gray base with bold color accents for each region
- Statistics as visual stories — bars, gauges, and radar charts integrated into the bracket
- Asymmetric layout with the bracket as the central interactive element
- Region-coded color system for instant visual orientation

**Color Philosophy:** Clean white (#FFFFFF) base with a dark slate (#1E2A3A) for text. Four distinct region colors: East (Royal Blue #1D4ED8), West (Forest Green #15803D), South (Crimson #B91C1C), Midwest (Amber #D97706). The palette is bold and purposeful — each region has its own identity.

**Layout Paradigm:** Full-width bracket as the hero section. Below: stats dashboard with KenPom charts, upset probability meters, and team comparison cards. Top navigation tabs switch between Bracket View, Stats Dashboard, and Upset Picks.

**Signature Elements:**
1. Region-colored seed badges with gradient fills
2. KenPom efficiency radar charts on team hover/click
3. Win probability progress bars in each matchup card

**Interaction Philosophy:** Click any team to see their full statistical profile. Hover matchups to see win probability. Tabs navigate between bracket, stats, and analysis views.

**Animation:** Smooth fade-in for bracket sections. Stats bars animate on scroll. Region colors pulse when their section is active.

**Typography System:** "Oswald" for team names and headings (bold, condensed, athletic), "Nunito Sans" for body and stats (clean, readable), "JetBrains Mono" for KenPom numbers.
</text>
